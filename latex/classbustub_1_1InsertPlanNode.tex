\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classbustub_1_1InsertPlanNode}{Insert\+Plan\+Node}} identifies a table into which tuples are inserted.

The values to be inserted are either embedded into the \mbox{\hyperlink{classbustub_1_1InsertPlanNode}{Insert\+Plan\+Node}} itself, i.\+e. a \char`\"{}raw insert\char`\"{}, or will come from the child of the node.

N\+O\+TE\+: To simplify the assignment, \mbox{\hyperlink{classbustub_1_1InsertPlanNode}{Insert\+Plan\+Node}} has at most one child.
