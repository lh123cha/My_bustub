\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{classbustub_1_1UpdatePlanNode}{Update\+Plan\+Node}} identifies a table that should be updated. The tuple(s) to be updated come from the child of the \mbox{\hyperlink{classbustub_1_1UpdateExecutor}{Update\+Executor}}.

N\+O\+TE\+: To simplify the assignment, \mbox{\hyperlink{classbustub_1_1UpdatePlanNode}{Update\+Plan\+Node}} has at most one child.
